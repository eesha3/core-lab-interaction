Tapiola Metro Station, Helsinki, Finland

Tapiola station is the metro’s second western terminal station and a key terminal for feeder traffic. The white backlit glass walls enhance the station’s atmosphere as an open and high space that expands towards the ticket sales level. The platform area is encircled by an urban platform serving the pedestrian connection between Tapiola Park and the south side of Merituulentie.

At the station platform, commuters are greeted by the sculpture “Emma leaves a trace” by Kim Simonsson. The Tapiola metro station was designed by APRT Arkkitehtityöhuone Artto Palo Rossi Tikka Oy.
