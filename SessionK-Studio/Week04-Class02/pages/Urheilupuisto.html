Urheilupuisto Metro Station, Helsinki, Finland

Urheilupuisto metro station is situated in Niittymaa, at the northern edge of Jousenpuisto Park, south of the sports grounds. The themes of the station are energy, motion, youth, strength, growth and colour. Unlike other West Metro stations, Urheilupuisto metro station is not constructed in bedrock. The station building is designed to allow daylight to reach the escalators all the way to the platform level.
