Toledo Metro Station, Naples, Italy

The Toledo Metro Station in Naples, Italy was opened in September 2012. The station was designed by the Catalan architect Oscar Tusquets Blanca and its main feature is the blue Bisazza mosaic which covers the walls and floors of the station giving you a feeling of being underwater. This project is a part of Napoli's Metro Art Station Circuit which includes various installations by some of the most talented artists.

Each level of the station is designed around a different color representing a different theme related to the city. The walls and floor of the atrium on the first level are black, recalling the asphalt of contemporary civilization.
